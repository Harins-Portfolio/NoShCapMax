## 1. Executive Commercial Summary
Unscheduled appointment vacancies result in severe clinical capacity wastage, underutilized medical personnel, and artificially inflated patient waiting lines. When **20.2%** of an organization's booking volume fails to materialize without prior notification, it introduces structural operational loss.

This repository charts a comprehensive, data-driven evolution from foundational business framing to predictive probability classification modeling, engineered to protect daily operational revenue.
